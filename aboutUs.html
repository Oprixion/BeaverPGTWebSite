Our CEO

Experiences nearly 25 years in oil field activities of which directly involved and contributed in Geology engineering included: Potential petroleum evaluation for Petrovietnam project in Vietnam / Tusnia / Uzbekistan / Myanmar. Founder and established of Beaver Petrogeotech Co. Ltd in Canada

Why are we doing this?

Over the past 30 years, petroleum geologists has gone from field-based science to 2D, and to 3D simulation. However, when they map oil reservoir, having the plan for drilling and production, they face with a lot of uncertainties. With a limited data, a single presentation of one oil field will have high probability of being inaccurate. History has shown there are oil fields which have billions of dollars invested but operating at only few percentages of the capacity. Indeed, with the current geological workflow, it is very easy for billions of dollars investment to go wasted.

What do we do?

That is why we come up with Petro GeoTech, the AI geological data analysis software system. The system will extract the trends of complicated geological datasets, build millions of reservoir scenarios in a short period of time, so that companies can make economic decisions along a reservoir life cycle. So instead of having a single presentation, our system will give geologists range of scenarios, and test whether that range is efficient enough to move forward, strongly supporting the investment decision of the company. Furthermore, the software will keep updating and automatically learning from all of the past projects of experienced geologists to improve its own intelligence, to produce better simulations in the future.
